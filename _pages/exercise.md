# Exercise: 

&nbsp;

*Time: 8-75m*

&nbsp;

&nbsp;
&nbsp;
&nbsp;

### Get Moving:
- Walk
- Jog, run or bike
- A certain amount of pushups (you can put your knees on the floor), squats and crunches
